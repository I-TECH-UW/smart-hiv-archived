34. HIVI.clientHistorySummary
Definition

With interoperable systems, the provider receiving the referral should be able to access the clients health record digitally. However, in the absence of this, the referral provider should receive a summary of the clients health records that includes the clients history, medications, medications prescribed or dispensed, reported issues and concerns, and any other relevant clinical information the health care provider had already obtained.

ShortClient history summary
Control1..1
Typestring
Primitive ValueThis primitive element may be present, or absent, or replaced by an extension
